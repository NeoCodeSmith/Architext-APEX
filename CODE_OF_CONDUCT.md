# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Architext APEX is a technical standards project. All participation — issues, PRs, discussions — is governed by one rule:

**Be direct, be accurate, be professional.**

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Critique ideas, not people
- Back claims with evidence or documented failure modes
- Accept that a constraint may be correct even if it is inconvenient
- Disagree in writing, with reasoning

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind
- Dismissing concerns without engaging their substance
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the standard, or off-topic content
- Deliberately introducing ambiguity to soften constraints

---

## Enforcement

Violations will result in:

1. **First instance** — comment removed, private warning issued
2. **Second instance** — temporary block from the repository
3. **Serious violations** — permanent ban without warning

The maintainer has final authority on enforcement decisions.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any other forum associated with this repository.

---

## Contact

To report a violation, contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.
